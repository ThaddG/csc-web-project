MBDTF

Road To Album Release

The VMA incident that started it all.

Kanye's stealing of the mic from Taylor Swift during the 2009 Video Music Awards caused him immediate backlash from media.

The Recording in Honolulu

The backlash resulted in Kanye escaping to Hawaii to take a break from the media, recording (shortly), and cancelling a tour. Also leading to the recording of MBDTF in the Avex Honolulu Studio, with select producers and other artists being flown in.

Never See Me Again

After the VMA incident but not before leaving for Hawaii Kanye recorded the song "Never See Me Again" (a personal favorite). I included this song because knowing the context of the event and surrounding backlash makes the song even better.
